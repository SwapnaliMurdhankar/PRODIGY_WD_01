50 years of serving communities

Celebrating 50 years of serving communities, Cofee House continues to be more than just a coffee shop; it's a cultural phenomenon, a gathering place, and a global community builder. From its humble beginnings in Seattle to its now-iconic presence in nearly every corner of the world,Cofee House has played a significant role in shaping the way we enjoy coffee and connect with one another.

The company has taken significant strides in fostering an inclusive environment for both employees and customers, demonstrating that it values the uniqueness of each individual.
